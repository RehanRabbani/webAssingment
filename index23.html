Food mania is the oldest and largest fast food resturant.the spaciality of this resturant is that you can try diffreent food with minimum cost.Bella Vita boasts an impressive menu created for those with a mature palette. From traditional Cafe offerings like sandwiches and salads to unique delicacies from the Italian and continental world, Bella Vita has carved a niche for itself in the Karachi cafe scene. Bella Vita also has a diverse breakfast menu which offers everything from pancakes and waffles to traditional items like omelettes and non traditional items like steak and eggs & Portuguese eggs.
